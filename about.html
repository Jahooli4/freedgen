About FreedGen

Michael Freed

Founder, FreedGen Ltd

Michael has over 15 years of experience working in renewable energy construction across the UK, Europe, and internationally.

He has contributed to the delivery of nearly 1 gigawatt of solar capacity, working across a wide range of roles including:

  • Construction management
  • Solar installation
  • Quality Control
  • Site Supervision
  • Drone monitoring and reporting

His experience includes involvement in large-scale utility solar projects, including projects of over 300 MW in Poland and Spain.

FreedGen was created to bring this real, on-site experience into a structured service that improves quality, visibility, and accountability across renewable energy projects.

Our Story

FreedGen was founded to solve a growing problem in renewable energy construction - a lack of independent oversight and real visibility on site./p>

As solar projects scale rapidly across the UK and Europe, construction teams are under increasing pressure to deliver quickly. This often leads to inconsistent quality, communication gaps, and limited insight for developers and investors.

FreedGen was built to bridge that gap.

By combining hands-on construction experience with modern technology, we provide clear, unbiased reporting that reflects what is actually happening on site - not just what is being reported.

FreedGen was created to bring this real, on-site experience into a structured service that improves quality, visibility, and accountability across renewable energy projects.

Our Approach

FreedGen takes a practical, no-nonsense approach to construction oversight.

We don’t rely on assumptions or second-hand reporting. We focus on what is actually happening on site.

By combining:

  • Independent site auditing
  • Drone-based monitoring
  • Detailed reporting

We provide a clear, accurate picture of project progress and quality - helping clients make informed decisions at every stage.

Partner with us to bring clarity, accountability, and quality to your project.
